About My City, Your City

During my trips, I try my best to talk with other lone travellers just like me, or local people.

All of them begin with "Hi, my name is Vu.", followed by stories exchanged about our cultures "You know, in Vietnam, we normally...".

The more we talk, the more we understand about each other's culture and gradually develop our love towards other countries.

Therefore, I would like to take it a little bit further to establish a small site, where anyone can publish anything at any time (preferably to be positive) about their country for everyone else to read

And that is how I come up with

My City, Your City
